# Coastal Banking System - User Manual

Welcome to the Coastal Banking System. This guide will help you navigate the application and perform your daily tasks efficiently.

## 1. Getting Started

### Login
1.  Navigate to the application URL.
2.  Enter your **Email Address** and **Password**.
3.  **OTP Verification**: If this is your first time logging in or if you are on a new device, an OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your registered phone number via SMS. Enter the code to verify your identity.

## 2. Manager Role Guide

As a Manager, you have full oversight of branch operations.

### Overview Dashboard
-   **Top Stats**: View System Uptime, Transactions Today, and API Speed.
-   **Staff Performance**: A leaderboard showing the most efficient staff members.
-   **Pending Approvals**: A list of Loans, Refunds, or Requests waiting for your action. Click "Review" to process them.

### User Management
-   **Create User**: Go to "Staff Users" -> "Create New User". Fill in details and verify the phone via OTP.
-   **Staff List**: View and manage existing staff accounts.

### Financial Approvals
-   **Loans**: Navigate to the "Loan Apps" tab. Review applicant details, credit score (if available), and approve or reject the loan.
-   **Cash Flow**: Monitor the `Cash Flow` tab for a real-time graph of inflows vs. outflows.

### Reports
-   **Generate**: Go to "Statements" or "Expenses".
-   **Format**: You can download reports in PDF or CSV format.

## 3. Cashier Role Guide

As a Cashier, your focus is on processing customer transactions and managing your cash drawer.

### Cash Drawer Management
-   **Start of Shift**: You MUST "Open Drawer". Enter the cash count for each denomination you are starting with.
-   **During Shift**: All cash transactions are recorded against your open drawer session.
-   **End of Shift**: Select "Close Drawer". Count your physical cash and enter the amounts. The system will calculate any **Variance** (Physical Cash vs. System Expected).

### Processing Transactions
-   **Deposit/Withdrawal**: Use the "Transactions" tab.
    -   Enter Account Number (System looks up customer).
    -   Enter Amount.
    -   Confirm details with customer.
-   **Check Deposit**: Navigate to "Check Deposits". Upload front/back images of the check and enter routing info.

## 4. Mobile Banker (Field) Guide

Designed for agents in the field collecting deposits.

### Field Tools
-   **Visits**: View your scheduled client visits for the day. Check in/out of locations (GPS tracked).
-   **Clients**: Access your assigned client list. view their balances and recent activity.
-   **Messaging**: Chat directly with the branch manager for support.

### Collections
-   **Record Collection**: From a Client profile, click "Collect".
-   **Receipt**: The system generates a digital receipt which can be SMS'd to the client.

---

## 5. Troubleshooting & Support

-   **Forgot Password?**: Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. You will receive an SMS/Email to reset it.
-   **System Error**: If you see a "500 Error" or "Network Error", check your internet connection first. If it persists, contact IT Support.
-   **Fraud Alert**: If you suspect suspicious activity, go to the "Security" tab (if available) or notify a Manager immediately.
